WHAT TO DO IF YOU'RE FAT:

The first thing what you have to do is obviously go do some exercises to lose weight

1. Start with cardio exercises like running on a treadmill, cycling or running. This helps lose you some weight but not entirely.

2. Because number 2 is about nutrition on when you want to lose weight. You have to eat less calories than you burn. This is the most important thing

Eat foods like tuna, shrimp or greek yoghurt to gain muscle and lose fat.

WHAT TO DO IF YOU'RE SKINNY:

1. Start with strength training exercises like weight lifting, push-ups, and squats. This helps build muscle mass.

2. Nutrition is key when trying to gain weight. You need to consume more calories than you burn.

Eat foods like eggs, milk, and lean meats to gain healthy weight.

WHAT TO DO IF YOU WANT TO BECOME STRONGER:

1. Focus on compound exercises like deadlifts, bench presses, and squats. These exercises work multiple muscle groups.

2. Ensure you have a balanced diet rich in protein, carbohydrates, and fats to support muscle growth.

Include foods like eggs, chicken breast, and salmon in your diet.
